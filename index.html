01 / PIPELINE

From raw packets to a verdict

Every stage is a module you can read, test, and swap out independently.

  • loadDownloads NSL-KDD or loads a local copy into labeled DataFrames. src/data_loader.py
  • cleanStrips duplicates, maps 39 fine-grained attack labels into 5 categories. src/preprocessing.py
  • encodeLabel-encodes protocol/service/flag; unseen test-time categories fall back safely instead of crashing.
  • scaleStandardizes numeric features — fit on train, applied to test, no leakage.
  • trainFits Random Forest, SVM, and KNN with class-balanced weighting. src/train.py
  • evaluateAccuracy, precision, recall, F1, false-positive rate, confusion matrix. src/evaluate.py
  • predictLoads a saved model + its exact preprocessing artifacts to classify new traffic. src/predict.py
02 / MODELS

Three classifiers, one benchmark

No single algorithm wins every metric — that's why the pipeline trains and reports on all three side by side.

DEFAULT

Random Forest

Class-weighted, 150 trees. Robust baseline that handles feature importance well and resists overfitting on noisy traffic features.

HIGH-DIM

SVM (RBF kernel)

Effective in the high-dimensional feature space NSL-KDD produces after encoding. Slower to train, often competitive on recall.

BASELINE

K-Nearest Neighbors

The simplest possible comparison point — if a fancier model can't beat KNN by a wide margin, that's a signal worth investigating.
